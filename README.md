## Cookie Thief

A fast paced singleplayer 8bit game developed with sdl2 thats heavily inspired by 1984 Bombjack by Michitaka Tsuruta as well as 2015 Downwell by Ojiro Fumoto.
Where you are a girl who loves cookies from a famous bakery a little too much and one day you steal their secret formula... only to be chased down after getting caught.
